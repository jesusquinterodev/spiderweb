History

Who is Peter Parker?

He is a young orphan born in Queens, New York, who lives with his Aunt May and Uncle Ben. He studies at Midtown Highschool and is characterized by being funny, kind, optimistic, smart and witty.

The Beginning

He acquires his powers after being bitten by a genetically modified spider at Oscorp Industries during a school visit. The spider was part of a project to combine animal DNA with human DNA.

What is happening!?

After the bite, Peter does not immediately assimilate either the changes taking place in his body or his new abilities. His world is turned upside down as he begins his process of self-awareness.

Prototype Suit

After beginning to master his powers he tries to use them to keep the neighborhood safe, but he knows he must keep his identity secret to avoid reprisals against his loved ones. Improvised costumes, but they served their purpose.

Final Suit

A sweater and a jogging suit weren't a real long-term solution, so a good suit had to be found. In some universes, Parker brings out his sewing skills, in others... let's just say Tony Stark made the task easier.

Agility

Spider-Man has incredible agility that allows him to move very quickly and catch his enemies off guard. In addition, with this same agility he can dodge any object that is being used against him.

Super Strength

He is capable of exerting force and lifting weights beyond what is physically possible for an ordinary human being. According to some comics, he can lift up to 10 tons.

Spider Sense

It is a pre-cognitive ability that warns of impending danger. This extrasensory awareness warns Peter several hundredths of a second before the danger occurs (falls, bullets or even undefined dangers).

Wall Climbing

He has the ability to adhere to any surface, thanks to the "micro-spikes" that grow on Peter's skin after the spider's bite.

Web-Shooting

From his wrists he launches webs that resist more than 10 tons. In the comics, he does it through a technological device, but Tobey Maguire's Peter Parker, in his trilogy, produces organic webbing.

Motto

"With great power comes great responsibility."
- Uncle Ben
